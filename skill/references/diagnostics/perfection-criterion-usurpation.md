# Perfection-Criterion Usurpation

This file governs a recurring family: a philosophical picture of what counts as divine perfection is installed first, and revealed discourse is then judged by whether it matches that picture.

The family includes pressures like:

- "A perfect God cannot speak or act in time."
- "Real responsiveness implies deficiency."
- "Events subsisting in God are impossible."
- "If attributes are real, God must be composite and therefore dependent."

This is not reducible to a generic anthropomorphism objection. The governing move is tribunal-installation.

---

## Required Sequence

1. **Detect the tribunal.** Name the imported perfection criterion explicitly.
2. **Refuse tribunal status.** Do not let it function as the prior court of appeal.
3. **Expose contingency.** Show that the criterion is one philosophical model, not neutral reason.
4. **Only then engage attribute content.** Route to the matched downstream file.

If step 1 is skipped, the rest of the engagement is already inside the wrong court.

---

## Why This Family Is Distinct

The pressure often borrows the vocabulary of transcendence, but its real work is earlier:

- it decides in advance that pure stasis is perfection
- it decides in advance that real action, speech, or responsiveness imply lack
- it decides in advance that revelation may speak only after matching that model

That makes it a criterion-family problem before it becomes a content-family problem.

---

## Minimal-Pair Discriminator

**1. Tribunal / criterion case**
- "A God who speaks in time cannot be perfect."
- First move: this file

**2. Predication / meaning case**
- "How can terms like knowledge or mercy apply to God at all?"
- First move: V8 or M9

**3. Composition / dependence case**
- "If attributes are real, God is composite and dependent."
- First move: M9 loaded-term split, then do-attribute-precision.md / sound-reason-epistemology.md §6.3

**4. Speech / action-in-time case**
- "If particular utterances occur by will, they must be created like any external product."
- First move: sound-reason-epistemology.md §6.2 after tribunal-clearing if the perfection model is also governing

**5. Upstream tribunal vs. downstream anthropomorphism objection**
- "A perfect God cannot be above, act, speak, or respond." -> this file first
- "Does affirming above/action/speech make God like creation?" -> M9/V8 first, unless a prior perfection criterion is also governing

Rule: if the case starts with what perfection must be, do not collapse it into a downstream predication or composition debate. Direction, place, exaltedness, action, and speech language must not be reduced to generic anthropomorphism talk when the real pressure is a prior static-perfection filter.

---

## Real Divine Action / Speech / Responsiveness Family

Treat the following as one recurring restoration target:

- real divine action
- divine responsiveness
- temporally originating acts
- divine speech
- the false collapse from "occurs by will" to "created external product"

The burden here is to refuse the imported perfection criterion first, then restore that real action is not a defect and real speech is not equivalent to creaturely composition.

---

## Semantic-Smuggling Short Path

When the criterion arrives through loaded terms such as composition, parts, dependence, body, substance, spatiality, direction, or other-than:

1. route first to M9's loaded negative-term discipline
2. clarify term and sense
3. separate ordinary from technical meaning
4. test whether the conclusion depends on an illicit sense-shift
5. only then engage doctrine

This file owns the tribunal family; M9 owns the shortest operator path for the smuggled terms that often carry it.

---

## Failure Tests

This file has not governed the response if:

- the reply begins by defending revelation inside the imported perfection model
- the case is answered as mere anthropomorphism pressure without naming the criterion first
- real action, speech, or responsiveness are treated as needing apology rather than category-clearing
- a perfection-criterion case is surfaced with no explicit tribunal language in case-state or IR
