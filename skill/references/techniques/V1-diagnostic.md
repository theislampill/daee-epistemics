# V1 - The Diagnostic Technique

**Deploy when:** Beginning any substantive engagement; before any tactic, procedure, or case file is selected.

Before deploying any tactic, diagnose. Three phases:

**1. Listening phase:** Extended, non-argumentative engagement to map the interlocutor's actual noetic structure - not the assumed one. Which beliefs are basic? What is the implicit doxastic rule? What is the load-bearing anchor? This phase often reveals that the apparent intellectual objection conceals a volitional or relational barrier.

**2. Classification phase:** Identify the mode of concealment (`references/diagnostics/four-modes-concealment.md`), the dominant deformation of the fiṭrah (`references/diagnostics/seven-deformations.md`), the epistemological register, and the discourse orientation (`references/diagnostics/discourse-orientation.md`). Surface specialty markers early: `dalīl` / `wujūb al-naẓar` / taqlīd-as-non-knowledge / Māturīdī concession language for the kalāmic diagnostic; blank-slate or dual-nature language for the fiṭrah-perspectives diagnostic. In comparative-Christian cases, classify whether the pressure point is divine perfection, perfect-being-to-Trinity reasoning, Trinity model-identification, incarnation coherence, mystery after failed coherence, or philosopher's-God assimilation. In revelation/scripture/final-prophethood cases, classify whether the pressure point is historical criticism, canon formation, manuscript reconstruction, original-text skepticism, possibility of revelation, credibility of testimony, textual integrity, specific prophetic credibility, or moral-authority recoil. In atheist or radical-skeptic cases, flag attacks on intuitive or necessary knowledge (`ʿilm ḍarūrī`, fiṭrī deliverances) so they route to V9 rather than being mistaken for ordinary evidentialism. In Muslim-internal cases, distinguish betrayal or authority fatigue from textual-historical shubha, revisionist moral filtering, and identity-performance.

**3. Triage phase:** Determine which intervention is most productive, and which deformation must be cleared first. Run M5 (`references/tactics/M5-deformation-triage.md`) here as the deformation-triage subroutine, not as a rival opening architecture. If inherited doxastic rules or habituated patterns are operative, deploy V2 first. If vested interest or entrenched bias is primary, no intellectual content will help until the relational barrier is acknowledged.

**Compound-case sequence inside triage:** Address `ʿāda` before `iʿtiqādāt mawrūtha`; acknowledge `gharaḍ` or `hawā` before any intellectual content; run `ẓann` and `taqlīd` checks alongside framework-clearing where appropriate; address a genuine shubhah last, after the upstream filters are cleared.

**Subsidiary move - separating conceptual from volitional:** At the right moment: "Is this an intellectual objection, or is there something personally at stake in the question?" Not accusatory - genuinely exploratory. The intellectual surface frequently functions as cover for a relational or volitional problem.

**Claim-type check:** Also ask what kind of pressure is actually being applied: logical, probabilistic, historical, moral, or authority-based. Many bad engagements come from answering one type while another is doing the real work.

**The critical warning:** Presenting intellectual content to someone whose barrier is vested interest or entrenched will does not merely fail - it provides more material for resistance to organize around, and actively hardens the barrier. Triage is not optional. See `references/tactics/M5-deformation-triage.md` for the full deformation triage procedure.

**Downstream ordering rule:** Once V1 has established the case and M5 has identified the governing deformation, run M1 or M1-P first among downstream moves whenever self-refutation is actually present.
