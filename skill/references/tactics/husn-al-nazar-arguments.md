# Ḥusn al-Naẓar - Inferential Arguments for the Impaired Fiṭrah

Load when `ḥusn al-naẓar` has already been selected and the framework has been cleared enough for inferential work. These are not primary means to theistic belief; they are secondary instruments for clearing the path back to fiṭrah-operation when the fiṭrah is too impaired to respond directly to signs.

This file supplies inferential content for the secondary `naẓar ʿaqlī` pathway. It remains downstream of:

1. framework-clearing
2. proof-family audit
3. any needed causal-series classification

Always sequence correctly:

- V2 - clear the framework
- M1 - self-refutation check
- `proof-method-audit.md` - classify what the proof family assumes and what it actually establishes
- `causal-series-taxonomy.md` - when regress / infinity / series classification is doing the work
- deploy the argument calibrated to this interlocutor
- return to V5 / R2 as soon as the intellectual obstacle is cleared

These arguments are not an argument bank. They are secondary inferential routes whose role is limited by the audit files above.

---

## Cosmological - Temporal Origination

Use when the interlocutor accepts causal reasoning and the impossibility of an actual infinite regress in the relevant sense. Do not use this section as the classifier for whether the regress is simultaneous, successive, or merely numerical; that belongs upstream in `causal-series-taxonomy.md`.

Core point: temporally originated reality is not self-explanatory. A causal series of originated things does not become self-sufficient merely by being extended backward.

---

## Explanatory Dependence / Contingency

Use when the issue is explanatory dependence rather than temporal origination. Audit first whether necessity/contingency language is functioning as imported proof grammar rather than neutral reason. If the grammar itself is the live issue, stay with `proof-method-audit.md` before loading this section.

Core point: explanatory deferral is not explanation. A chain of dependent items does not explain itself merely by being long.

---

## Teleological / Sign-Directed Design

Use when the interlocutor is open to inference to intelligence from order, calibration, and purposive structure. Keep the mode sign-directed rather than turning every sign into a heavy syllogism.

Core point: order is most effective here when it directs attention rather than trying to replace fiṭrah with abstraction.

---

## Moral Grounding

Use when moral facts are being affirmed while the stated framework lacks a stable ground for them.

Core point: objective moral reality is more at home in a reality grounded by a personal source than in a reality where value is left hanging as brute fact.

---

## Consciousness and Normative Orientation

Use when the interlocutor is philosophically trained and the specifically normative character of consciousness is live.

Core point: what matters here is not bare experience alone, but the truth-oriented, morally responsive, gratitude-capable structure of human consciousness.

---

## After the Argument

Return immediately to fiṭrah-activation mode. The argument cleared a path; it did not become the final resting place of the case.
