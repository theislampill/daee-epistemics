# M5 — Deformation Triage

**Type:** Meta-tactic — V1 triage subroutine; standalone only for narrow local sorting
**Deploy when:** During V1's triage phase, or in a narrow single-exchange case where the live question is already reduced to which deformation governs the next move.

Before selecting any downstream tactic, identify which of the seven deformations is primary. The response appropriate for inherited doxastic rules (iʿtiqādāt mawrūtha) is categorically different from the response appropriate for vested interest (gharaḍ). Deploying argument against an entrenched will (hawā) deepens resistance. Deploying relational engagement against a genuine shubhah leaves the intellectual problem untouched.

**The triage sequence:**
1. Run the noetic reading checklist (`references/diagnostics/noetic-reading-checklist.md`) to surface the operative noetic structure.
2. Ask whether the upstream block is a general inherited filter, a habituated pattern, or a single faulty presupposition (`mushābara fāsida`) that must be targeted surgically.
3. Identify which of the seven deformations (`references/diagnostics/seven-deformations.md`) is primary.
4. Check discourse orientation (`references/diagnostics/discourse-orientation.md`) — if not truth-seeking, intellectual engagement may be the wrong instrument entirely. If concealment or orientation are still unclear, hand the case back to V1 rather than forcing a tactic choice.
5. Select the tactic appropriate to the primary deformation.

**The critical warning:** Presenting intellectual content to someone whose barrier is vested interest or hawā does not merely fail — it provides more material for resistance to organize around and actively hardens the barrier. The most common failure mode in sustained epistemological engagement is failing to triage — supplying arguments when the barrier is relational.

**The compound case:** Most real interlocutors exhibit more than one deformation. Sequence the interventions from outside in:
- ʿāda before iʿtiqādāt mawrūtha
- gharaḍ or hawā before any intellectual content
- ẓann and taqlīd checks alongside framework-clearing where appropriate
- genuine shubhah last, after the upstream filters are cleared

**Router guardrails:** M5 helps identify the upstream obstruction; it does not replace the family
router once the live case-type is clear.
- If the presenting pressure is manuscript, canon, preservation, original-text, or text-history
  destabilization, route through V10 and the matched RT case before letting M5 become the opening
  architecture.
- If the presenting pressure is Trinity, incarnation coherence, perfect-being-to-Trinity, or
  philosopher's-God pressure, route through V8 and the matched DO case after deformation sorting.
- If the presenting pressure is a discursive attack on necessary knowledge itself, route to V9
  rather than treating the issue as generic shubhah management.

**Category C caution:** The Category C note in `references/diagnostics/seven-deformations.md` is not a normal first-line routing label for surface discourse. It only governs suspension of pressure after direct evidence of cognitive inaccessibility or extended failure of every matched instrument.
